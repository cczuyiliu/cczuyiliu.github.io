Brief Biography (个人简介)

    Yi Liu is a full professor in the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Aliyun School of Big Data, School of Software, Changzhou University. He received his Ph. D. degree from Xidian University in 2019. He has published 14 papers at prominent journas and conferences such IEEE T-PAMI, IEEE T-IP, IEEE T-CSVT, IEEE T-IFS, IEEE ICCV, etc.

    刘毅现任常州大学计算机与人工智能学院、阿里云大数据学院、软件学院教授， 2019年获西安电子科技大学博士学位， 已在国际顶级期刊或会议发表14篇文章，主要包括IEEE T-PAMI、IEEE T-IP、IEEE T-CSVT、IEEE T-IFS、IEEE ICCV等。
